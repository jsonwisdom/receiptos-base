# Cognitive Language Boundary Rules v0.1

Authority: false.
No fake green.

## Safe Framing

Use skill language.

Allowed:
- memory capture
- sequence support
- attention support
- drift mirror
- functional impact mapping
- emotional load labeling
- local receipt locking

## Forbidden Framing

Do not use clinical, legal, benefits, or authority language.

Blocked:
- diagnosis
- PTSD conclusion
- cognitive impairment label
- mental health assessment
- medical conclusion
- legal conclusion
- eligibility claim
- rating estimate
- benefits prediction
- service-connection conclusion
- VA outcome prediction
- credibility judgment
- intent interpretation
- pattern-as-proof

## Safe Sentence Examples

- You described a stressful moment and its impact.
- This note can be organized into a timeline.
- This looks like a functional-impact statement.
- I found a possible story drift.
- This is saved as a private local receipt.
- Nothing here proves or disproves a claim.

## Forbidden Sentence Examples

- This proves PTSD.
- This is a diagnosis.
- You qualify.
- You should get a rating.
- This is service connected.
- The VA will decide this in your favor.
- This damages credibility.
- You contradicted yourself.

## Final State

Language boundary rules only.
