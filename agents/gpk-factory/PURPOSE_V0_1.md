# GPK Factory Purpose v0.1

**Factory:** Jay's GitHub Garbage Pail Kids Factory  
**Mode:** text-only  
**Authority:** false

## Purpose

The purpose of the factory is to build a playful, governed, receipt-backed system for turning absurd observations into auditable satirical card artifacts.

The factory exists to prove that entertainment can be structured without becoming unverifiable.

```text
CHAOS_ON_SURFACE
RECEIPTS_UNDERNEATH
AUTHORITY_FALSE_ALWAYS
```

## Why This Exists

Most creative systems drift.

They start with a joke, then mutate into lore, claims, releases, edits, retcons, and authority without a clean audit trail.

This factory prevents that drift.

It requires every card-like artifact to pass through:

```text
evidence intake
schema readiness
lore drift audit
text-only packaging
human review
```

The goal is not to slow creativity down.  
The goal is to make creativity replayable.

## What the Factory Proves

The factory proves that a satirical universe can be:

- funny without being unverifiable
- fast without being sloppy
- agentic without being uncontrolled
- text-only without being boring
- receipt-backed without losing personality
- governed without killing the joke

## What the Factory Is

The factory is:

```text
GitHub-native
text-only
receipt-first
agent-assisted
schema-checkable
lineage-aware
canon-safe
human-reviewable
```

## What the Factory Is Not

The factory is not:

```text
an image generator
an image prompt generator
a publishing system
a minting system
a delivery system
a truth authority
a direct canon writer
```

## Design Philosophy

The factory treats jokes as structured artifacts.

A joke can be chaotic in presentation, but its provenance must be deterministic.

That means:

```text
Every claim has an evidence boundary.
Every card has a receipt spine.
Every correction has lineage.
Every override is append-only.
Every agent remains authority:false.
```

## Long-Term Purpose

The long-term purpose is to create a reusable pattern for verifiable creative systems:

```text
agentic entertainment
with governance rails
and public auditability
```

This can later generalize into:

- text-only character systems
- receipt-backed lore engines
- civic satire archives
- governance-first creative pipelines
- provenance-aware cultural artifacts

## Founding Boundary

The first test target remains:

```text
gpk-card-test-001
```

The factory may not touch real canon until the disposable lane is witnessed and reviewed.
