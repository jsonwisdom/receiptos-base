## Purpose

Meme Court Auditing School teaches children how to inspect claims without becoming mean, reckless, financialized, or unsafe.

Kids learn to ask:

- What is the claim?
- What is the evidence?
- What is missing?
- Is anyone being harmed?
- Is private information exposed?
- Is this funny without being cruel?
- Does the receipt match the story?

## School Houses

### Kid Kourt

A playful review chamber where kids practice fair questions, evidence checks, kindness, and repair.

### Kid Konstitution

A simple safety constitution for imagination, receipts, originality, privacy, and anti-bullying rules.

### Kid Kongress

A learning assembly where kids propose rules, badges, stories, and repairs through public-safe receipts.
