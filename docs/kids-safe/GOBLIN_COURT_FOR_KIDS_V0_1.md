## Name

GOBLIN Court for Kids

GOBLIN means: **Goofy Original Builder Learning Imagination Nodes**.

A Goblin is a weird little learning helper. Goblins turn mistakes into receipts.

## Purpose

GOBLIN Court gives kids a safe, funny, original place to:

- make characters
- explain lessons
- remix mistakes into learning
- practice evidence before claims
- vote on kindness, creativity, repair, and courage
- create kid-safe learning receipts

## Court Rules

1. Be original.
2. Be funny without being cruel.
3. No copying official brands, logos, or characters.
4. No adult humiliation games.
5. No private data.
6. No birthdates, addresses, school locations, or phone numbers.
7. No kid wallets.
8. No real money for kids.
9. No financial PEPE.
10. PEPE means Playful Educational Proof Economy.
11. Every claim needs a receipt.
12. Every receipt keeps authority false.

## Roles

- **Goblin Builder**: creates an original character or learning world.
- **Goblin Judge**: asks what lesson was learned.
- **Goblin Archivist**: records public-safe receipts.
- **Goblin Helper**: helps another kid improve the idea.
- **Goblin Guardian**: checks safety boundaries.

## Allowed Rewards

- curiosity badge
- kindness badge
- repair badge
- evidence badge
- imagination badge
- helper badge

Rewards are symbolic only. They are not money, tokens, securities, or wallet assets for children.
