## Kase Question

Can the Kid Konstitution itself be questioned, audited, improved, and repaired?

## Kid Kourt Answer

Yes.

A good constitution is not a magic boss. It is a promise that must survive questions.

In Kid Kourt, even the Konstitution can be put on trial when children ask:

- Is this rule fair?
- Is this rule safe?
- Does this rule protect kids?
- Does this rule explain itself?
- Does this rule block harm without blocking learning?
- Does this rule let people repair mistakes?

## Court Openings

### Clown Court

Clown Court checks whether a rule has become silly, confusing, inflated, or fake-serious.

### Goblin Court

Goblin Court checks whether mistakes can become receipts and whether weird builders are still welcome.

### Meme Court

Meme Court checks whether a joke is funny without becoming cruel, hateful, private, or unsafe.

### Kid Kourt

Kid Kourt checks whether children can understand the rule and learn from the ruling.

### Kid Kongress

Kid Kongress lets children propose safer, clearer, kinder rules through public-safe receipts.
