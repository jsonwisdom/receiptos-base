## Purpose

Rhythm & Soul Protocols help children learn through movement, music, pattern, feeling, breath, story, and repair.

The goal is not performance pressure. The goal is safe expression, pattern learning, emotional literacy, kindness, and receipts before claims.

AI may help suggest age-safe activities, but AI is not the authority.

## Learning Protocols

### Rhythm Protocol

Children practice patterns through clapping, tapping, counting, stepping, call-and-response, and simple repetition.

### Soul Protocol

Children practice naming feelings, listening to others, repairing mistakes, and expressing care without shame.

### Movement Protocol

Children use safe body movement to learn letters, numbers, shapes, timing, and calm-down routines.

### Call & Response Protocol

Children learn turn-taking:

- teacher asks
- child answers
- group repeats safely
- repair happens kindly

### Storybeat Protocol

Children turn a story into beats:

1. beginning
2. problem
3. feeling
4. helper
5. repair
6. receipt

### Quiet Beat Protocol

Children learn that rest, silence, breath, and calm are part of the song.
