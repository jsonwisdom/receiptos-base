## Purpose

Kid Kindergarten 2026 is an early-learning surface for safe, playful, AI-enabled education.

Children can ask questions, make stories, draw worlds, explain feelings, practice letters and numbers, and learn how to use receipts before claims.

AI may help, but AI is not the authority.

## Learning Rooms

- Story Room: children create original stories.
- Number Room: children practice counting, patterns, and simple math.
- Feeling Room: children name feelings and practice repair.
- Evidence Room: children learn what a receipt is.
- Goblin Room: children turn mistakes into learning helpers.
- Meme Room: children learn funny without cruelty.
- Kid Kourt Room: children practice fair questions and safe rulings.

## AI-Enabled System Rules

AI may:

- ask gentle questions
- explain at kindergarten level
- suggest safe activities
- help make original characters
- help sort learning receipts
- help identify missing evidence
- help suggest repair paths

AI may not:

- become authority
- make private decisions for a child
- collect private data
- ask for addresses, school locations, phone numbers, or birthdates
- create child wallet flows
- create real-money participation for children
- financialize PEPE
- shame, bully, or humiliate children

## Kid Kuorum Requirement

Every Kid Kindergarten ruling or lesson must include:

1. Child question
2. Evidence or missing-evidence note
3. Safety boundary check
4. Repair or learning path

No Kuorum means do not rule.

## Hard Boundaries

- No child wallets.
- No real money for kids.
- No financial PEPE.
- PEPE means Playful Educational Proof Economy.
- No private data.
- No birthdates.
- No phone numbers.
- No addresses.
- No school locations.
- No bullying.
- No humiliation games.
- No official IP claims.
- No canonical authority claim.

## Ruling

Kid Kindergarten 2026 is open as an R&D-safe, AI-enabled early-learning system.

AI can accelerate learning, receipts, and repair, but cannot become authority.
